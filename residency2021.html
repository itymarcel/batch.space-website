Change Makers 2021

Calling all change makers! The Batch.Space residency program is offering three months of free membership for projects that are pushing boundaries.
Who?
Wether your change is environmental, social or technological. We are looking for individuals who are creating a positive impact.

If you have an idea get in touch!
How?
Please send your website/portfolio to hello@batch.space along with an A4 written proposal of your project answering the following:
  • What is your project?
  • How does your project create positive change?
  • How will you use the facilities available to you at Batch.Space?
  • How would this residency further your career?
