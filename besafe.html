How do we keep our community safe?

We are currently operating at reduced capacity and ask anyone that can work from home to please do so. However we understand this is sometimes just not possible (particularly for those needing making the facilities for their work).
Our current measures
Batch.Space will remain open 24/7 and be maintaining social distancing measures, extra-sanitation, new access instructions and sign-posting to ensure our site remains Covid-Secure so we can support our community, staff and visitors alike.
  • All of our members must wear a face covering in the communal areas of the building which we are happy to provide.
  • There is hand sanitizer available at every entrance/exit of the space as well as in the meeting rooms and kitchen area.
  • We have a guest sign in system in place using our booking platform to ensure that we do not run into an unsafe capacity.
  • All communal areas of the Hackney Depot building are cleaned and sanitized on a regular basis.
Updates
We are currently monitoring all official gov guidance and will keep members informed as we know more.
